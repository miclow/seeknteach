About Me

I'm originally from Alaska, USA. I came to Australia in 2012.

I have been teaching English as a second language for over 10 years in Australia. I have taught business English and normal every day English. If you are studying for the TOEFL or IELTS test I can help. I know how to incorporate phrasal verbs and idioms into the conversation to get my students to learn how to speak English like a native speaker.

Interests: I love to be outdoors, hiking, biking, swimming or sailing. I live near a lake in Canada so I get to go sailing a lot. I am also interested in psychology, history, geography and other cultures. I love talking to people and I love teaching. In fact, I love talking to students from all over the world.
